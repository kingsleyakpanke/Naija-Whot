How Naija Whot was founded

Since 2025

Naija Whot started as a passion project to preserve and share the joy of the classic Nigerian Whot card game. Built by a Nigerian developer who grew up playing Whot on neighborhood evenings and school breaks, the project began as a lightweight web experiment and evolved into a full mobile-first game with rules tuned to local house play.

Our mission

Make Whot accessible to anyone with a phone — no installs, no accounts. A faithful rule set, elegant visuals, and offline-friendly gameplay are the pillars of Naija Whot.

How to play

Quick rules
  1. Match a shape or number: Play a card that matches the top card's shape or number.
  2. WHOT (20): Play a WHOT to choose a shape or number the next players must follow.
  3. Pick rules (Nigeria): 2 = Pick 2 (defended only by 2), 5 = Pick 3 (defended only by 5). These stacks are separate.
  4. Hold On & Suspension: 1 = Hold On (play again); 8 = Suspension (skip next player).
  5. General Market: 14 causes other players to draw (standard house rules may vary).

Tip: In our game you can choose difficulty (Easy / Medium / Hard) and the AI will play differently based on the level chosen.
