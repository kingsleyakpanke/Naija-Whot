Whot tactics — intermediate tips

This guide gives practical tips for players who know the basics and want to win more often. It includes stack management, WHOT timing, and when to hold on.

Stack awareness

Keep track of pick-2 and pick-3 stacks. If a pick stack is active, decide whether you can defend with the same number; otherwise draw early and focus on discarding high-value cards.

WHOT timing

Use WHOT to force shapes that favour your hand. If you have multiple cards of a shape, switching the demand can give you multiple plays in a row.

Hold-on strategy

1 (hold-on) is powerful when combined with a strong hand; use it to chain plays and create pressure on opponents.
