About Naija Whot

Naija Whot is a project built to faithfully reproduce the Nigerian Whot experience in the browser. It focuses on mobile-first performance and common Nigerian house rules.

Mission

To keep the social and cultural experience of Whot alive and make it accessible on phones without installs or accounts.
